The college library is not a part of college but heart of the institution since 1972 with a collection of 4074 Books at present the central library of the college has very rich collection of 33735 books (volumes) and 20000 titles on various subjects like commerce, science and arts etc. Total area of the library is 3193 sq.ft.

Rules & Regulations

Admission to the library is strictly against identity card, student should submit identity card whenever staff demand.
Students, Teachers and administrative staff are entitled to enroll themselves to the library.
Silence shall be strictly observed in the library, please converse in low voice and politely.
Tobacco products, smoking and electronic devices like mobile, iPads etc are strictly prohibited.
Books, journals, news papers and rare books should be used carefully and also do not mark in the library book.
Any damage to the book or to the furniture of the library has to be made good by the student.
Each student has enroll library member will be issued with three book for home.

Vision

To work with faculty in integrating information skill, knowledge of information Sources, and the use of technology in accessing needed information to strengthen their research
To provide instruction and assistance in the effective use of learning resources.
The Library intends to incorporate the latest technology and adopt user friendly approach towards students and faculty.

Mission

To help rural Student, to enrich their knowledge, to create better world for themselves.
To encourage student to read beyond the requirements of the curriculum & inculcate reading habit.
The Library intends to offer comprehensive services related to dissemination of knowledge.
To acquire, organize and update the library collection to support teaching- learning process.
